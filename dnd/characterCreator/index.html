Info

  • Each stat is generated by rolling 4 d6, taking the 3 highest rolls. There are 6 stats.

  • Use the dropdown menu to choose which stat you want to assign to which roll.

  • Dnd Character Roller is good for a DM who wants to quickly create new NPCs and download their stats for later

  • You can use the tab key to navitage inputs. Use arrow keys to choose your stat, or enter to open the dropdown.
